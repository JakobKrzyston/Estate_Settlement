{# ── LEGAL RIGHTS NOTICE ── #}

Please be advised that, pursuant to 12 C.F.R. § 1024.38, you are required to promptly identify and communicate with any successor in interest upon notice of a borrower’s death and to provide a written description of documents required to confirm that person’s identity and ownership interest in the property. I am formally presenting myself as a potential successor in interest and request that you initiate this process immediately.

Please further note that, under the Garn–St. Germain Depository Institutions Act (12 U.S.C. § 1701j-3), you may not exercise any due-on-sale clause as a result of this transfer by reason of death to a relative of the borrower. The loan should continue to be serviced on its existing terms during the estate settlement process.
